1.Name the three group into which chemical equilibrium can be classified.
Ans .
Chemical equilibrium can be classified into three groups
(i)
The reaction that proceeds nearly to completion and only negligible concentrations of the
reactants are left.
(ii)
The reactions in which only small amounts of products are formed and most of the
reactants remain unchanged at equilibrium stage.
The reactions in which the concentrations of the reactants and products are comparable,
when the system is in equilibrium.
