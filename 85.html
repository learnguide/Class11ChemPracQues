16..If Qc > Kc, what would be the type of reaction?
Ans .
If Qc > Kc, the reaction will proceed in the direction of the reactants (reverse reactions)
17.What inference you get when Qc = Kc?
Ans .
If Qc = Kc, the reaction mixture is already at equilibrium.
18.State Le chatelier’s principle.
Ans .
It states that a change in any of the factors that determine the equilibrium conditions of
a system will cause the system to change in such a manner so as to reduce or to counteract
the effect
of the change.
19.Can a catalyst change the position of equilibrium in a reaction?
Ans .
No, a catalyst cannot change the position of equilibrium in a chemical reaction. A
catalyst,
however, affects the rate of reaction.
