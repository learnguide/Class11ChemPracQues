30.How is alkyne prepared from vicinal dihalides?
Ans.
Vicinal dihalides on treatment with alcoholic potassium hydroxide undergo
dehydrohalogenation. One molecule of hydrogen halide is eliminated to form alkenyl halide
which on treatment with sodiumamide gives alkynes.
