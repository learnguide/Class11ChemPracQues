41.What do you mean by delocalization?
Ans.
Delocalisation – Delocalisation implies that pairs of bonding electrons extend over three
or more atoms and belong to the whole molecule. Delocalized π-orbitals are much larger
than the localized π-orbitals and are therefore more stable.
