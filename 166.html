14.Can a catalyst change the position of equilibrium in a reaction?
Ans .
No, a catalyst cannot change the position of equilibrium in a chemical reaction. A
catalyst affects the rate of reaction.
15.If Qc < Kc, when we continuously remove the product, what would be the direction of
the reaction?
Ans.
Continuous removal of a product maintains Qc at a value less than Kc and reaction
continues to move in the forward direction.
