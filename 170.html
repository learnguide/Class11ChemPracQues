Hydrocarbons
2 Marks Questions
1.The boiling point of hydrocarbons decreases with increase in branching. Give reason.
Ans.
Branching result into a more compact (nearly spherical) structure. This reduces the
effective surface area and hence the strength of the Vander wall’s forces, thereby leading to a
decrease in the boiling point.
2.Unsaturated compounds undergo addition reactions. Why?
Ans.
Unsaturated hydrocarbon compounds contain carbon – carbon double or triple bonds.
The π-bond is multiple bond is unstable and therefore addition takes place across the
multiple bonds.
3.To which category of compounds does cyclohexane belong?

Saturated alicyclic hydrocarbons.
