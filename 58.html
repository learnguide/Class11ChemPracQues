Because their critical temperature is lower than room temperature and gases cannot be
liquefied above the critical temperature even by applying very high pressure.

7.Define absolute zero temperature.
Ans.
The lowest hypothetical or imaginary temperature at which gases are supposed to
occupy zero volume is called Absolute zero.
8.Define an ideal gas.
Ans.
A gas which obeys the ideal gas equation (PV = nRT) at all temperature and pressure is
called an ideal gas.
