General characteristics of the long form of Periodic table :-
(i)
There are in all 18 vertical columns i.e. 18 groups in the long form periodic table.
(ii)
There are groups numbered from 1 to 18 from the left.
(iii)
There are seven horizontal rows called periods.
(iv)
The elements of groups 1, 2 and 13 to 17 are called main group elements.
(v)
The elements of group 3 to 12 are called transition elements.
