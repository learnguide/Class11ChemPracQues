17.How do metals react in a period?
Ans.
The tendency of an element to lose electrons decreases in going from left to right in a
period. Thus the reactivity of metals goes on decreasing in a period from left right.
18.How do metals react in a group?
Ans.
the tendency to lose electrons increases as we go down a group so the reactivity of
metals increases down the group.
19.What is an amphoteric oxide?
Ans.
Oxides which behave as acids with bases and as a base with an acid are called
amphoteric oxide.
20.Define a neutral oxide

Neutral oxides have no acidic or basic properties.
