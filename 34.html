2 Marks Questions
1.How did Mendeleev arrange the elements?
Ans.
Mendeleev arranged elements in horizontal rows and vertical columns of a table in
order of their increasing atomic weights in such a way that the elements with similar
properties occupied the same vertical column or group.
2.Name the two elements whose existence and properties were predicted by Mendeleev
though they did not exist then.
Ans.
Mendeleev predicted not only the existence of gallium and germanium, but also
described some of their general physical properties.
