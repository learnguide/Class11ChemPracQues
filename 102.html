1.Define oxidation reaction?
Ans.
Addition of oxygen /electronegative element to a substance or removal of hydrogen /
electropositive element from a substance.
2.Define reduction reaction?
Ans.
Removal of oxygen / electronegative element form a substance or addition of hydrogen /
electropositive element to a substance.

4.What is the most essential conditions that must be satisfied in a redox reaction?
Ans.
In a redox reaction, the total number of electrons lost by the reducing agent must be
equal to the number of electrons gained by the oxidizing agent.
