17.Define one atomic mass unit (amu).
Ans:
One atomic mass unit (amu) is defined as a mass exactly equal to one – twelfth the mass
of one carbon – 12 atom.
18.What is formula mass?
Ans:
When a substance does not contain discrete molecules as their constituent units and
have a three dimensional structure, formula mass is used to calculate molecular mass which
is sum of all the atomic masses of atom present in the formula.
