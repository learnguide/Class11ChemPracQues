9.Why metals like potassium and sodium can not be extracted by reduction of their
oxides by carbon?
Ans.
Potassium and sodium are strong electropositive metals and have great affinity for
oxygen than that of carbon. Hence they Cannot be extracted from their oxides by reduction
with carbon.
