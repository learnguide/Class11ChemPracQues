2.Give the main characteristics of isotopes.
Ans.
Since, the isotopes have the same electronic configuration, they have almost the same
chemical properties. The only difference is in their rates of reactions, mainly due to their
different enthalpy of bond dissociation. However, in physical properly of these isotopes
differ considerably due to their large mass differences.
