16.How are Van der waal’s constants ‘a’ and ‘b’ related to the tendency to liquefy?
Ans.
The Van der waal’s constant ‘a’ is a measure of intermolecular attractions. Therefore,
the value of ‘a’ reflects the tendency of the gas to liquefy. The gas having larger value of ‘a’
will liquefy more easily.
