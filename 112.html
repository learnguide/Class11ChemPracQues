8.What is water – gas shift reaction?
Ans.
The production of dihyrogen can be increased by reacting carbon monoxide of syn gas
mixtures with steam in the presence of iron chromate as catalyst.
This is called water gas – shift reaction.
9.Why is dihydrogen gas not preferred in balloons?
Ans.
Dihydrogen is the lightest gas and should have been used in balloons. But it is not
preferred due to its highly combustible nature.
