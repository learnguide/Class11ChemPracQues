16.Why does CO
2
have a linear shape with no dipole moment
Ans.
In CO
2
molecule carbon atom undergoes sp hybridization. Two sp hybridized orbital of
carbon atom overlap with two p-orbital’s of oxygen atoms to make two sigma bonds while
other two electrons of carbon atom are involved in pπ – pπ bonding with oxygen atom. This
results in its linear shape [with both c-o bond of equal length (115 pm)] with no dipole
moment.
