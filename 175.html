16.Why the addition of inert gas does does not change the equilibrium?
Ans.
It is because the addition of an inert gas at constant volume does not change the partial
pressures or the molar concentrations of the substance involved in the reaction.
17.The equilibrium constant of a reaction increases with rise in temperature. Is the
reaction exo – or endothermic?
Ans.
The equilibrium constant increases with a rise in temperature. Therefore, the reaction
is endothermic.
