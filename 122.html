3.Why are melting and boiling points of alkali metals low?
Ans.
The melting and boiling points of the alkali metals are low indicating weak metallic
bonding due to the presence of only a single valence electron in them.

The diagonal relationship is due to the similarity in ionic sizes and /or charge / radius
ratio of the elements.
