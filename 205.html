4.Discuss the water pollution caused by industrial water?
Ans.
The compounds of lead, mercury, Cd, Ni, Co, Zn etc which are the products of chemical
reactions, carried in the industrial units, pollute water to a large extent and are responsible
for many disease. Mercury leads to minimarts disease, lead poisoning leads to many
deformities. In addition, these substances adds to the soil and harmfully affect the plant
growth and the whole soil biotic system. Both ground water and water bodies are polluted
due to chemical reactions known as leaching.
