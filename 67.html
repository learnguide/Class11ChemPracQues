14.Why does viscosity of liquids decrease as the temperature is raised?
Ans.
Viscosity of liquids decreases as the temperature rises because at high temperatures
molecules have high kinetic energy and can overcome the intermolecular forces to slip over
one another between the layers.
15.What is the effect of temperature on
(i) density
(ii) vapors pressure of a liquid?
Ans.(i)
The volume increases, with the increase of temperature. Therefore, density
decreases with the rise of temperature.
(ii)
As the temperature of a liquid is increased, the vapors pressure increases.
